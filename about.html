Making the World a Better Place, One Good Deed at a Time

Join us on our journey as we strive to make a meaningful difference in the world. Through acts of kindness, generosity, and compassion, we're dedicated to creating positive change, one good deed at a time. Together, let's build a brighter future for everyone.

Our Mission

We are a team of passionate people committed to making the world a better place one good deed at a time. GiveMe.kz is an online platform where everyone can contribute to supporting those who need help. Our mission is to bring together those who want to help and those who need help, creating a path to kindness and well-being.

Contribution and Support

On our platform, you can share your unwanted items, knowing that they will find a new life and bring joy to those in need. Clothes, shoes, books, toys - every item can become a source of smiles and support for those who are in difficult life situations.

Transparency and Honesty

We strive for transparency and honesty in our work. All items received through our platform are carefully checked for quality and usability before they reach the hands of those in need.

Partnerships with Orphanages and Charities

We also collaborate closely with orphanages and charitable organizations to ensure that our assistance effectively reaches those who need it most, making a tangible difference in their lives.
